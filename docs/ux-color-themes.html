My Recommendation

Theme 1 (Slate Professional) or Theme 2 (Ocean Trust) are your best options for the "calm confidence" fintech vibe.

Slate Professional is more neutral and timeless - like Stripe. Works for any market.
Ocean Trust leans into financial trust with deeper blues - like Mercury. Feels more distinctly "fintech."

Theme 3 (Sage Growth) is unique but green-heavy UI can be tricky when you also use green for positive numbers.
Theme 4 (Warm Minimal) is approachable but may feel less "serious" for investment decisions.
